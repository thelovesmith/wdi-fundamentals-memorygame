Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card geme in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

This is my first game created via coding.
It is easy, fun, and quick to play.
It is the perfect game for someone who needs a short distraction or is very bored
